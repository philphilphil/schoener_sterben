## Top Picks

The strongest names across all lists — working on two frequencies, communicating editorial posture in a single breath, short, leading with curiosity/warmth, and viable as URLs:

1. **Generalprobe** — The dress rehearsal. Implies "we're figuring this out together, not performing expertise." Insiders know the term; newcomers get the vibe. Clean URL.
2. **Pausenplausch** — The intermission chat — exactly what the blog does: turning to a friend and gushing. Warm, social, unmistakably German.
3. **Opernluft** — Fresh air + opera. The antidote to stuffiness. Simple, memorable, optimistic.
4. **Fermatenglück** — The happiness of a held pause. Poetic for insiders, intriguing for newcomers. Beautiful compound.
5. **Vorhangluft** — The air when the curtain rises. That moment of anticipation. Evocative and original.
6. **Waldweben** — Wagner's forest murmurs. Works with or without Ring knowledge — pure nature imagery, listening, wonder.
7. **Herzgeflüster** — Heart-whisper. Wagner-sourced, intimate, personal. Perfect for a warm blog voice.
8. **Attacca** — "Continue without pause." Musical term that doubles as a statement of intent — keep going, don't stop, next piece immediately. Short, punchy, domain-friendly.
9. **Wonnemond** — Wagner's "month of bliss." Gorgeous word, works standalone, signals beauty without pretension.
10. **Hörprobe** — Listening test / audition. Implies "try this, listen to this" — the blog's core gesture. Clean, short, inviting.
11. **Prima Vista** — At first sight/reading. Suggests freshness, first encounters, discovery. Works across languages.
12. **Stehplatzmut** — The courage of standing room. Being there even without the best seat. Beautiful metaphor for non-expert enthusiasm.
13. **Arienrausch** — Aria intoxication. Physical, enthusiastic, unashamed love of the art form.
14. **Feuerzauber** — Wagner's fire magic. Spectacular, warm, transformative. Works as pure imagery even without the Ring.
15. **Dramatisch, aber ok** — "Dramatic, but fine." Self-aware, funny, modern. Perfect tagline energy as a name.
